Know Your Ballot

Kentucky is a closed primary — you vote only in the Democratic primary. Most Scott County county offices are R-only this cycle, but you do have a contested D primary for State House 62 and (in Magistrate District 6) for Constable, plus nonpartisan Mayor & City Council if you live in Georgetown.

Polls open 6 AM – 6 PM. If in line at 6 PM, you must be allowed to vote.

D88 · NOT ON YOUR D BALLOT

State House District 88

Northeast Scott County — Newtown, Leesburg, parts of east Georgetown.

Alisha Chaffin (D) was the only Democrat to file, but a Scott County circuit judge ruled May 13, 2026 that her declaration was improperly signed and disqualified her. The 88th District has no Democratic candidate on the May 19 ballot.

Republican incumbent Vanessa Grossl will run unopposed in November (she defeated Cherlynn Stevenson in 2024). The R primary is Grossl uncontested.

Source: WKYT May 13, 2026; Emerge Kentucky alumna profile
