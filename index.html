Know Your Ballot

Kentucky is a closed primary — you vote only in the Democratic primary. In Scott County, no local county offices have Democratic candidates on the primary ballot. Your choices are federal races and nonpartisan local races.

Polls open 6 AM – 6 PM. If in line at 6 PM, you must be allowed to vote.
